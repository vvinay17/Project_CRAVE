Crave International is a startup based out of Hyderabad. The main motto of CRAVE International is to provide "QUALITY SERVICE WITH PASSION"and satisfy their clients at their level best. Every project which crave does will be taken as its first project in order to show their complete dedication towards their work and passion.

We conduct events like Hackathons, Workshops, Corporate Events, Seminars, Conferences, Non-Technical Events, cultural events, concerts and many more. We have also conducted events for Government of India.We train the students on many topics like IoT, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and many more. Mentoring their projects and taking them to global level is our main aim.
